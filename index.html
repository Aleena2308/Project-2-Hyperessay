Candies We all Grew Up With

Feeling groovy? Explore the sweet world of Candy and take a trip down memory lane!
Discover the old candy you use to enjoy, and unearth the delicious vintage candy you dream about!

M&Ms

M&M's are an old fashioned candy favourite! They first came out in 1941, and are still as popular today as ever. M&M's Candy is sold in over 100 countries and there are over 40 million individual M&M Candy made every day. The original M&M's Candy was bright candy-coated milk chocolate. The second introduction was M&M's with peanuts. Now M&M Candy has extensively expanded its flavours. M&M's Candy now comes in peanut butter, pretzel, dark chocolate, caramel, almond and even strawberry. Any and every one of M&M's flavours is delicious. So go on, try them all, and let them "melt in your mouth, not in your hands".

BlowPop

A lollipop with bubblegum in the middle! Whoever came up with this idea was pure genius! Childhood is just not complete without indulging in these double whammy lollipops. Blow Pop comes in so many flavours it's hard to just choose one! Some of their more modern flavours are Sour Apple, Black Ice, Blue Razz,What-A-Melon and Candy Corn. These retro lollipops are just hard to beat! First the delicious lollipop, then the chewy bubblegum! These were the very first gum filled lollipops in the world! They've been around since the early 1970s. Groovy Baby!

Starbursts

"Unexplainably juicy" yes, the perfect description of a Starburst.These soft chewy, taffy morsels of goodness have been around since 1967. Starburst was once called "Opal Fruits'' when it was first made in England, but upon its arrival here to North America, the name was fittingly changed to Starburst.I can't imagine eating a candy called Opal Fruits, Starburst is where it's at. The name is so fitting for the exciting, intense burst of flavour in every bite.Starburst are great for sharing, or trying to bribe someone. They come in a great little convenient package, just the right size for pockets too!

Swedish Fish

You might not like tuna fish but these Swedish Fish are hard to resist! Always a go-to candy at the corner store, easy to stuff your brown paper bag with, even easier to eat. Swedish Fish Candy came out in the 1950s, and remains a retro candy favourite. Their sweet fruity flavour is sort of a mystery. Some say cherry, others say raspberry. Actually they say the flavour of Swedish Fish is more of an exotic berry called lingonberry. Whatever the berry, we'll take them. This retro candy is a must-have for any age. Who knew that these retro fish were so exotic!

Dots

Dots Candy is colourful and bright gumdrops candy made with a soft jelly-like texture and fantastically fruity flavours. Open wide for five intense flavours of Lime, Cherry, Strawberry, Lemon and Orange. Dots candy is an explosion of fabulous-tasting fruitiness that you won't soon forget!

Reeses

There's a reason that Reese Peanut Butter Cups are the number one selling candy brand in North America.Nothing in the whole world is quite as perfect as the combination of peanut butter and chocolate.Reese's Peanut Butter Cups also come in some new variations like white chocolate, crunchy, king size, and even dark chocolate. Reese's also makes Reese's Pieces, another old fashion candy we love to indulge in. They now have Reese's Pieces filled peanut butter cups! The Reese's brand is truly iconic, coming out in 1928, and is recognized worldwide.

Skittles

A brilliant, sweet rainbow of flavours! Skittles is a must-have. Every morsel of this chewy, mouth-watering candy is not only bright and colourful but is matched with an intense fruity taste that is second to none.Skittles Candy gave us their most delicious rainbow of flavours in 1979. Now there are many more besides the originals. These include Skittles Sour, Skittles Brightside, Skittles Wildberry, and even Skittles Sweet Heat. Time to make up for a childhood with only the Skittles Original. What about you, which one will you try next?

Nerds

They might be tiny, but they're oh so crunchy, tangy and delicious! A box of Nerds Candy comes with two separate compartments and two different flavours all in one small and colourful box. Nerds Candy is inspired by Willy Wonka and The Chocolate Factory. They are a magical tiny burst of explosive flavour, from very sweet, to extremely sour. Nerds Candy are especially good when you have a great big mouthful of them and then try to talk. You can even mix and match the flavours of the box, or just eat one side at a time. Nerds are a retro candy from the 1970s and are still so groovy!

Twizzlers

Our list would not be complete without some classic licorice candy. Twizzlers are the most popular licorice candy that is consumed in North America. Red or black licorice, Nibs or the original, it's an old fashioned candy that the world certainly loves to eat. Twizzlers make one million miles of the stuff every year! That amounts to circling the globe 40 times! It's even a part of the famous boxer, Floyd Mayweather's pre-fight diet. Twizzlers also has the ability to be used as a straw! Just bite both ends off and voila, you have a straw that's environmentally friendly, not to mention delicious! Here's an interesting fact: only the black licorice from Twizzlers has real licorice extract in it.
